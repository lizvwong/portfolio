Tree Giant

Tree Giant (Rise of the Olympian)

Enemy giants resembling trees who roam Crete Valley.

Since these enemies had roots instead of legs, I had to figure out how they would move. In the end, I thought it would be interesting if they moved like an octopus.

Making the roots flow together yet uniquely was a fun challenge. I watched a lot of octopus-walking videos as my reference (even though I am scared of octopi).

Original concept art by Hannah Fu.
