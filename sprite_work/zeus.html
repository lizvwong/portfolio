Zeus

Zeus (Rise of the Olympian)

The protagonist of Rise of the Olympians, young Zeus. He is not yet the king of Gods and ruler of Olympus, but just a child of Cronus.

His chibi style was fun to draw! I experimented with many different pixel art drawing softwares while making Zeus. I also learned a lot about sprite art and animation while making him.

Lighting attack concept art by Hannah Fu.
Death animation concept art by Yve.
