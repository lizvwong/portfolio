Adastreia

Adastreia

In Greek mythology, Adastreia is one of the nymphs who nursed baby Zeus while he was hiding on Crete.

My first character sprite ever. Originally made for the indie game Rise of the Olympian but later removed because of a change in art direction.

Adastreia is one of my personal favorite character sprites I've created. I put a lot of effort into making her hair flowy and volumous when she moves.
