1000x1000

Click to listen to the original song.

A mini music video based on the song "1000x1000" by Mili with characters Genzuki Tojiro and Maa-chan from Nijisanji. Made in Adobe After Effects. Art by me.

I wanted to put them Genzuki and Maa into 1000x1000 because the lyrics resonate with their stories. Instead of the still image like in the original music video, I wanted to add simple depth and movement to the scene to make it feel more alive. I focused more heavily on the video editing rather than the drawing for this video.

The 3D effect (foreground and midground movement) was made on a 2D plane completely by accident, but I loved the effect so I kept it. Now I know a neat 3D trick!
