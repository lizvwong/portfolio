Sunshine is Brief

Lyrics:

"Let's forget about everything for just tonight.
Even if it's only a momentary shelter from the rain,
It's moonlight, gently burning—
An ephemeral dream glittering in the haze."

Lyrics translation from Releska

Click to listen to the original song.

A video project based on the song "Sunshine is Brief" by Kikuo ft. si_ku made in Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects.

I tried to capture the feeling of wanting to stay in a daydream using shaky footage of things I personally thought were daydream worthy: mostly birds (my favorite animal) and nature. Over 40 GB worth of videos were shot for scenes lasting only a few seconds. I brought my camera and tripod to my local county park, which attracted attention from hikers passing by. It was fun, though!

I also experimented with rotoscoping myself into the video, because like a dream, I imagine I'm there even though I'm not. I wanted to convey the feeling of being only half-there.
