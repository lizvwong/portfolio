Twin Moons

Click to listen to the original song.

A mini music video based on the song "Twin Moons" by Fuyuyakata with characters Genzuki Tojiro and Maa-chan from Nijisanji. Made in Adobe After Effects. Art by me.

After listening to Twin Moons, I found that the original characters in the music video reminded me of Genzuki and Maa, so I wanted to make a fan music video with them in it. Coincidentally, the lyrics fit their story as well.

This was my first attempt at making a Vocaloid-style music video, so I focused more on the video editing rather than the artwork. It was also my first time using After Effects, so I learned a lot, like syncing lyrics, by making this!
