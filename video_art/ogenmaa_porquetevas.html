Por Que Te Vas

Lyrics:

"You're leaving and my dreams go with you
You'll forget me, you'll forget me

Tell me then, how will I be
Without you?
Without you"

Lyrics translation from Genuis

Click to listen to the original song.

An animatic for the animation meme "Por Que Te Vas" featuring Genzuki Tojiro and Maa from Nijisanij.

This was my first time making a lip-sync animation and an animation meme. I felt the lyrics matched their story, which is why I made this.

It was an interesting experience because I had no idea what software to use, so I played around with a couple! This was also my first experience with Premiere Pro.
