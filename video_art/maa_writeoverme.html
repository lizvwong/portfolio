Write Over Me

Lyrics:

"Goodbye to those few irreversible words I hurled at you that day.
Goodbye, goodbye. They've become a mirage.
Goodbye to your warmth and the fleeting illusion.
Goodbye, goodbye. Are you judging me?
They were too heavy to carry.
My swollen memories are just like blisters after a burn.
I tried piercing them with a needle, but it was too thick to pierce.
Just like the back cover of a science textbook.
My torn ego was hydrolyzed.
Goodbye to my dear youth that I left behind.
Goodbye, goodbye. Our time together has been cut short.
Goodbye to you and the synopsis that I wrote in permanent marker.
In my head, the ending song just kept repeating itself."

Lyrics translation by Paino

Click to listen to the original song.

A fan-made music video of Write Over Me by Iyowa with the character Maa-chan from Nijisanji. Made in Adobe Premiere Pro. Art by me.

Making this was pretty straightforward but fun! The most interesting part was making the scribbled-out text effect. I ended up screenrecording myself actually scribbling out the text and overlaying it on the animation in Premiere Pro with a filter.

I decided to make this because I fell in love with the scribbled-out effect and wanted to recreate it myself! It adds a layer of depth to the simple animation.
