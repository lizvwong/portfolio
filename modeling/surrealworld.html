Surreal World

A world with a mix of 3D, 2D and real-life video. Made in Blender and Adobe After Effects.

I wanted to create an "unfinished" and unrealistic world where all sorts of things that shouldn't be together, coexist.

This was my first time using Blender and recording in a 3D space. Definetly a learning curve, but it was very enjoyable! Most of the 3D models were made by myself, with the exception of the rows of free-to-use apartment buildings. I used After Effects to rotoscope/mask my real-life videos.
