Wisteria

An illustration of Genzuki Tojiro from Nijisanji under a wisteria tree at sunset.

I wanted to draw an accurate wagasa (traditional Japanese umbrella), so I researched how they were made and browsed wagasa online stores. The umbrella drawn here actually exists in real life!

Playing around with the lighting and colors took a while but I am proud of the result!
